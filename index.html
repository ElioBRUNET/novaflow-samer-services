Service 01 · Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

What we actually do
when we run your Meta Ads.

Setup · 15–25h

Phase 1 · Setup (one-time)

Building the foundation

  • Create & configure Business Manager and Ad Account
  • Install Meta Pixel on your website (tracks visitors & conversions)
  • Define target audiences: age, location, interests, behaviours
  • Write 3–5 ad variations (copy + headlines)
  • Design or adapt ad visuals (images / video thumbnails)
  • Structure campaigns: awareness → consideration → conversion
  • Set bidding strategy, daily budgets, scheduling
  • Launch & verify tracking is working correctly

Monthly · 8–15h

Phase 2 · Management (monthly)

Making it perform

  • Weekly performance analysis (CTR, CPM, ROAS, CPA)
  • Turn off underperforming ads, scale what works
  • A/B test new audiences, creatives, messages
  • Retargeting: re-engage people who visited but didn’t convert
  • Lookalike audiences: find more people like your best customers
  • Monthly performance report with insights & next steps
  • Budget reallocation based on real results

Service 02 · Google Ads

Capturing people who are
already searching for you.

Setup · 10–20h

Phase 1 · Setup (one-time)

Keyword strategy & campaign build

  • Keyword research: what terms are your clients actually searching?
  • Competitor analysis: what are they bidding on?
  • Campaign structure: Search, Display, or both
  • Write ad copy for each keyword group
  • Configure negative keywords (stop wasting money on irrelevant clicks)
  • Set up conversion tracking (calls, form fills, appointments)
  • Geographic targeting for Switzerland

Monthly · 6–12h

Phase 2 · Management (monthly)

Optimising for real results

  • Bid adjustments: pay less for clicks that don’t convert
  • Add new negative keywords weekly (prevents budget waste)
  • Test new ad copy variations
  • Quality Score optimisation (better score = cheaper clicks)
  • Landing page recommendations to improve conversion rate
  • Monthly report: impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per lead

Service 03 · SEO (Search Engine Optimisation)

Getting found on Google
without paying per click.

Audit · 8–15h

Phase 1 · Audit & Technical Setup

Making your site rankable

  • Full SEO audit: speed, structure, errors, indexing issues
  • Keyword mapping: which page should rank for which term
  • Technical fixes: meta titles, descriptions, H1/H2, image alt tags
  • Google Search Console & Analytics setup
  • Local SEO: Google Business Profile (critical for Swiss healthcare)
  • Competitor gap analysis: what they rank for that you don’t

Monthly · 6–10h

Phase 2 · Monthly SEO Work

Building organic visibility

  • 1–2 optimised content pieces / blog articles per month
  • Link building: getting other sites to reference yours
  • Monitor keyword rankings and traffic evolution
  • Fix new technical issues as they arise
  • Monthly SEO report: rankings, organic traffic, wins
  • Results are compounding — grows every month
