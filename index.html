Service 01 · Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

Reaching the right people
before they search for you.

One-time · 15–25h

Setup

Building the foundation

  • Create & configure Business Manager and Ad Account
  • Install Meta Pixel on your website to track visitors and conversions
  • Define target audiences: age, location, interests, professional profile
  • Write 3–5 ad variations (headlines + body copy)
  • Design or adapt ad visuals for each placement
  • Build campaign structure: awareness → consideration → conversion
  • Configure bid strategy, daily budgets, and scheduling

Monthly · 8–15h

Monthly Management

Making your budget perform

  • Weekly analysis: CTR, CPM, cost per lead, ROAS
  • Stop underperforming ads, scale winning ones
  • A/B test new audiences, creatives, and messages continuously
  • Retargeting: re-engage people who visited but didn’t convert
  • Lookalike audiences: find more people like your best clients
  • Monthly performance report with insights and next steps

Service 02 · Google Ads

Capturing people who are
already looking for you.

One-time · 10–20h

Setup

Keyword strategy & campaign build

  • Keyword research: what terms are your clients actually typing into Google?
  • Competitor analysis: which keywords are they bidding on?
  • Campaign structure: Search, Display, or Performance Max
  • Write ad copy for each keyword group (3 variations minimum)
  • Negative keyword list: stop paying for irrelevant clicks from day one
  • Conversion tracking: calls, form fills, appointment bookings
  • Geographic targeting configured for Switzerland

Monthly · 6–12h

Monthly Management

Optimising for real results

  • Bid adjustments: pay less for clicks that historically don’t convert
  • Add negative keywords weekly to prevent wasted spend
  • Test new ad copy and landing page recommendations
  • Quality Score optimisation: a better score means cheaper clicks
  • Monthly report: impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per lead
  • Strategy adjustments based on seasonal demand and competition
